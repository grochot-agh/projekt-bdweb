How to play?

- Log in or create an acccount if you don't have one yet.
- Create a room or join an existing one. In both cases you will need a room name and password.
- Wait for other players to join.
- When everyone is ready, the room owner (the person who created it) will start the game.
- Three requirements will be given after the start of the game.
- Your task is to create a password (word) that meets these requirements.
- The results will be displayed at the end of each round that lasts 30s.
